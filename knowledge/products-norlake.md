# Norlake Product Knowledge

## Kold Locker Walk-Ins

RSG's flagship standard walk-in cooler/freezer line with the broadest configuration range in the industry.

| Spec | Value |
|---|---|
| Configurations | 14,000+ |
| Lead Time | Ships in 2 days |
| Insulation | Foamed-in-place polyurethane |
| Assembly | Cam-lock system |
| Models | Indoor and outdoor |
| Warranty | 15-year panel warranty; 18-month parts and labor |
| Installation | Nationwide Approved Service Provider network |

**Key Applications:** Restaurants, grocery stores, institutional kitchens, floral storage, beer caves, meat lockers.

**Proof Points:**
- 14,000+ configurations ensure virtually any standard need can be met off the shelf.
- 2-day shipping eliminates extended project delays for standard walk-in needs.
- 15-year panel warranty demonstrates long-term confidence in panel construction and insulation quality.
- 18-month parts and labor warranty is 50% longer than the 12-month industry standard.

**Insulation Detail:** "Soft nose" foamed-in-place polyurethane. Panels join with foam-to-foam joints that eliminate thermal bridges. Bonded to panel skins without adhesive.

**Competitive Advantages — Foamed-in-Place Polyurethane:**
- Superior thermal performance (higher R-values, lower energy costs)
- Better moisture resistance (closed-cell structure reduces mold/mildew risk)
- Greater structural strength (chemically bonded to skins, no adhesive)
- Foam-to-foam panel joints eliminate thermal bridging

## Capsule Pak ECO (Self-Contained Refrigeration)

Patented self-contained refrigeration system using R-290 natural refrigerant. Pre-charged, one-piece design with cord-and-plug electrical for simplified installation.

| Spec | Value |
|---|---|
| Refrigerant | R-290 (natural, GWP of 3) |
| Patent | U.S. Patent No. 11,859,885 |
| Design | One-piece, pre-charged |
| Electrical | Cord-and-plug, most models 115V |
| Controller | LogiTemp electronic (standard) |
| Max HP | 1.75 HP (self-contained limit) |
| Connectivity | Remote monitoring via Sitrad app (verify standard/optional per model) |
| Origin | Made in USA, ISO 9001:2015 certified |

**Footprint vs. Legacy:** Up to 28% shorter, up to 20% narrower.

**Energy Savings (Verified — Subway/Budderfly study):**

| Metric | Legacy R-404A | Capsule Pak ECO | Savings |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cooler (kWh/day) | 5.6 | 2.5 | 56% reduction |
| Freezer (kWh/day) | 11.3 | 5.6 | 51% reduction |
| Average | — | — | ~50% |

**Proof Points:**
- R-290 GWP of 3 vs. R-404A GWP of 3,922 — 99.9% reduction in global warming potential.
- Patented design (U.S. Patent No. 11,859,885) with exclusive latching system.
- RSG transitioned to R-290 self-contained systems nearly 3 years ahead of AIM Act deadline.
- Outdoor models include heater that prevents freezing in low ambient conditions (patent pending).

**IMPORTANT LIMITATION:** Self-contained systems limited to 1.75 HP maximum. Do NOT position R-290 as suitable for all walk-in applications. Larger installations require Split-Pak remote systems.

## LogiTemp Electronic Controllers

Patented electronic controller standard on Capsule Pak ECO and Split-Pak freezer systems (6 HP+).

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Energy Savings | Up to 27% vs. all-mechanical systems |
| Defrost | Demand Defrost (defrosts only as needed) |
| Data Logging | 30-day room and coil temperature logs |
| Connectivity | Wireless or Cat5 remote monitoring/programming |

**LogiTemp Plus Reverse Cycle Defrost (option on Split-Pak 6 HP+):**

| Metric | Electric Heater | Reverse Cycle | Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|
| Defrost energy | Baseline | Up to 80% less | 80% reduction |
| Freezer defrost time | 20-30 min | 3-5 min | ~85% faster |
| Cooler defrost time | 20-30 min | 1.5-2 min | ~93% faster |

**Standard Inclusion:**
- All Capsule Pak ECO: LogiTemp standard
- Split-Pak freezer 6 HP+: LogiTemp standard
- Split-Pak other: LogiTemp optional
