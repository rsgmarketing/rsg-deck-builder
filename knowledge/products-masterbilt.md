| Product Line | Description |

## Endless Glass Door Merchandisers

Modular glass door merchandiser line with industry-leading viewing area and shelf depth.

| Spec | Value |
|---|---|
| Models | BEM (refrigerator), BEL (freezer) |
| Door Configurations | 2-door to 5-door |
| Refrigeration | Remote or semi-self-contained |
| Shelf Depth | 23" deep cantilever (deepest standard available) |
| Shelves per Door | 5 |
| Modularity | Unlimited door line-ups |
| Complete Package | Endless CP (self-contained, single case) |

**Proof Points:**
- Largest glass door height and tallest interior case height in category.
- 23" deep cantilever shelves are the deepest standard shelves available.
- 200+ sq inches more viewing area per door than competitors.
- 100% of product pack-out space falls within the visible merchandising area.

**Competitive Advantages:**
- Front air curtain prevents warm air infiltration during door openings.
- Modular design allows unlimited door configurations for any store layout.
- LED lighting and electronic controller system standard.
- Endless CP option provides complete self-contained package for single case needs.

## Ice Cream Cabinets

All models use R-290 refrigerant and cold wall evaporator technology for even temperature without air blowing across product.

**Conventional Dipping Cabinets (DC Series):**

| Model | Display Capacity |
|---|---|
| DC-2S | 3 cans |
| DC-4S | 6 cans |
| DC-6S | 8 cans |
| DC-8S | 10 cans |
| DC-10D | 14 cans |
| DC-12D | 18 cans |

**Dipping-Display Cabinets (DD Series):** DD-26, DD-46, DD-66, DD-88 — Standard, Low Front Glass, Low Curved Front Glass variants.

**Refrigerated Rail Topping Centers (FLR Series):** FLR-60, FLR-80.

| Spec | Value |
|---|---|
| Refrigerant | R-290 |
| Evaporator | Cold wall with foamed-in-place refrigerant lines |
| Top Surface | Stainless steel |
| Interior | Galvanized steel |
| Electrical | 115V cord-and-plug |

**Competitive Advantages:**
- Cold wall evaporator provides even temperature distribution without air circulation across product.
- Foamed-in-place refrigerant lines ensure consistent cooling across the entire cabinet.
