# Refrigerant Regulatory Landscape

## AIM Act Overview

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Enacted By | EPA, December 2020 |
| Goal | 85% reduction in high-GWP HFCs by 2036 |
| 2026 Mandate | ALL new remote installs must use low-GWP refrigerants (GWP < 300) |

## Refrigerant Transition Map

| Category | Legacy (Being Phased Out) | Compliant Alternatives |
|---|---|---|
| Self-Contained | R-404A (GWP 3,922) | R-290 (GWP 3) |
| Remote | R-404A, R-407A, R-449A | R-454A, R-454C (A2L, mildly flammable) |

## RSG Compliance Position

- RSG transitioned self-contained systems to R-290 nearly 3 years ahead of the AIM Act deadline.
- All remote systems quoted for 2026+ installations use AIM Act-compliant refrigerants.
- Split-Pak ECO is the industry's first R-290 remote refrigeration system.

## Risk Messaging for Unprepared Operations

Specifications written today that are not compliant will face complications if installed after 12/31/2025:
- Project delays and change orders
- Business disruption during peak seasons
- Potential inability to source legacy refrigerants for service
