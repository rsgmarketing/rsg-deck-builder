## Competitive Landscape Overview

RSG competes in the commercial walk-in cooler/freezer market, glass door merchandiser market, and ice cream cabinet market. The walk-in market is the primary competitive battlefield.

### Walk-In Cooler/Freezer Competitors

#### US Cooler

**Market position:** Dominant in organic search visibility. Largest organic competitor in the walk-in keyword space.

**Strengths:**
- Strong SEO presence (ranked for many keywords RSG targets)
- Broad product line
- Established brand recognition in walk-in category

**RSG win themes vs. US Cooler:**
- RSG's 14,000+ configurations and 2-day shipping vs. their lead times
- 15-year panel warranty + 18-month parts and labor (vs. 12-month industry standard)
- Foamed-in-place polyurethane insulation vs. polystyrene (thermal performance, structural integrity, moisture resistance)
- R-290 Capsule Pak ECO technology (sustainability leadership)
- ISO 9001:2015 + ISO 14001:2015 dual certification

#### KPS Global

**Market position:** Competitor in both standard and custom walk-in segments.

**Strengths:**
- Custom configuration capabilities
- Established in larger/more complex installations

**RSG win themes vs. KPS Global:**
- Faster lead times on standard configurations (2-day shipping)
- Broader standard product range (14,000+ configs off the shelf)
- Integrated refrigeration solutions (Capsule Pak, Split-Pak, LogiTemp -- single source)
- Patented technology (R-290 self-contained, LogiTemp controllers)

#### Thermo-Kool

**Market position:** Regional competitor with focus on value segment.

**Strengths:**
- Competitive pricing
- Regional market presence

**RSG win themes vs. Thermo-Kool:**
- Manufacturing scale (660,000+ sq ft across two facilities)
- 15-year panel warranty + 18-month parts and labor (vs. 12-month industry standard)
- Technology differentiation (R-290, LogiTemp, Reverse Cycle Defrost)
- Nationwide service network
- ISO dual certification

#### Amerikooler

**Market position:** Growing competitor in walk-in space.

**Strengths:**
- Growing market presence
- Competitive lead times on some products

**RSG win themes vs. Amerikooler:**
- Deeper product portfolio (walk-ins + refrigeration + controllers)
- Heritage and track record (since 1938/1947)
- Patented technology platform
- Single-source walk-in + refrigeration + controller solution
- Broader dealer/service network

### Glass Door Merchandiser Competitors

The Endless glass door merchandiser line competes against major brands in the commercial display case market. RSG's key differentiators:

- Largest glass door height and tallest interior case height in category
- 23" deep cantilever shelves (deepest standard available)
- 200+ sq inches more viewing area per door than competitors
- 100% visible merchandising (no dead zones)
- Front air curtain technology
- Modular unlimited door configurations

### Ice Cream Cabinet Competitors

Master-Bilt ice cream cabinets compete in the commercial dipping/display cabinet market. Key differentiators:

- All R-290 refrigerant across the line
- Cold wall evaporator (even temperature without air blowing across product)
- Foamed-in-place refrigerant lines
- Full range: dipping (DC), dipping-display (DD), topping centers (FLR)

## RSG Category Win Themes

These are the overarching themes to use in customer-facing content (without naming competitors):

### 1. Single-Source Advantage

"Walk-in + refrigeration from one manufacturer. One warranty. One service call. One relationship."

- Most competitors manufacture panels only and purchase refrigeration from a third party. They are not refrigeration experts.
- RSG designs and manufactures both walk-in panels and refrigeration systems in-house
- Single-source = fewer compatibility issues, simpler warranty claims, one point of contact

### 2. Speed and Availability

"14,000+ standard configurations. Ships in 2 days."

- Many competitors quote 4-8 week lead times for standard configurations
- RSG's 2-day shipping on standard Kold Locker is a significant competitive advantage
- Fast-Trak handles custom needs when standard won't work

### 3. Sustainability Leadership

"R-290 natural refrigerant. 3 years ahead of the AIM Act deadline."

- RSG was first to market with R-290 self-contained systems
- Split-Pak ECO is industry's first R-290 remote system
- Competitors are still transitioning; RSG has years of field data
- 50% energy savings provide ROI argument alongside environmental benefit

### 4. Warranty Confidence

"15-year panel warranty. 18-month parts and labor. That's confidence you can measure in decades, not months."

- RSG walk-in panels carry a 15-year structural warranty AND 18-month parts and labor (industry standard is 12 months)
- Refrigeration systems carry a 12-month warranty with optional 5-year compressor warranty
- Dual warranty structure signals long-term manufacturing confidence and reduces specification risk

### 5. Technology That Proves Itself

"Patented. Tested. Verified by third parties."

- Capsule Pak ECO patent (U.S. Patent No. 11,859,885) -- not just a claim, a legal distinction
- Energy savings verified by independent Subway/Budderfly study
- ISO 9001:2015 + 14001:2015 -- quality AND environmental management
- LogiTemp data logging provides ongoing proof of performance
